Operations Orientation 02

Cargo

Most starships are equiped a series of cargo bays to provide for their long voyages between resupply points. Day to day operations onboard a starship require the use of materials for repairs and upkeep.

It is important to keep a close watch on cargo levels when at a resupply point, and to make note of which systems require repairing, and what the needed supplies are, so that you may make recommendations and bring aboard the required material.
