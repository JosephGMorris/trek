Tactical Orientation 06

Shields

Shields are a critical part of any tactical engagement; without them, the ship's hull is completely defenceless to any enemy fire, and would like not withstand any significant attack.

During combat, you will wish to keep a continual eyes on the shield status of your ship, to assit you comm and engineering team in moving power to affected systems, as well as orienting the ship in such a maner as to protect vulnerable systems.

The tactical officer is only capable of raising or lowering shields. More directly control on subsystems requires the assistance of the engineering officer.
