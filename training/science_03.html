Science Orientation 03

Detailed Scanning

A detailed scan is conducted on any object within range as a standard operating procedure. Not only is such a scan required to ascertain any potential transport sites, it is also required for the tactical officer to target any subsystem on the object in question, should the need arise.

During a scan, you will have the chance to observe lifesigns, cargo, and system status of the object being scanned. It is your duty to report if any of these readings are relevant or unexpected to the rest of the bridge crew.
