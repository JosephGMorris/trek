Ship Coordinates

The "mark" notation is used to describe elevation off of the plane, in an upward direction. This means that 45 degrees above and in front of the ship is expressed as 0 mark 125. 0 mark 250 is straight up, and 0 mark 750 is straight down. It is conventional to keep the orientation of the ship relative to the plane, and so there is no 0 mark 250 - 750. Instead, above and behind your position would be expressed as 500 mark 249.
