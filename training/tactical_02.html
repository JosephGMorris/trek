Tactical Orientation 02

Communications

Subspace communication allows faster-than-light information transfer and is a critical component of a functioning ship's bridge. As the bridge officer responsible for communications, you will be charged with hailing other vessels, reporting on any notifications from Starfleet, and coordinating actions with other star ships.

On the comms menu, you are able to see message history, and to transmit messages as required for the completion of your mission. Some traffic is routine banter and operational notification, while other communication is vital and relevant to the rest of the bridge's operation. It is your duty to pay attention and decide what is relevant.
