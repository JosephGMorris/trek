Tactical Orientation 07

Targeting

Before firing any weapon, it is important the targeting computer first has a lock. Any known target within weapons range can be targeted.

Subsystem targting becomes possible once a target has had a detailed scan completed by the science officer. This is useful if you wish to target specific components or systems onboard an enemy ship, allowing you to disable weapons, shielding, or any other component. Often it is good practice to target weapons fire if only to concentrate it and thus overcome any shielding around a ship.
