Conn Orientation 01

The conn station is responsible for starship navigation.

Conn's principle screens are:

Navigation, which displays your ships's position in the local sector or current system, as well as any tactical data the computer has collected in the system.

Warp Control, which allows you to set course and speed for impulse and warp drive systems.

Helm, or Thruster Control, which displays the immediate position of the ship, and is important for maneuvering and combat operations.

Conn's main screens can be accessed in the main screen selection window, in the upper left corner.

All of the Conn's screens can be sent to the mainviewer by selecting the Mainviewer control, in the bottom left.
