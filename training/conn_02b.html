Ship Coordinates

Rotation is stated in relative counter clockwise rotation, as a multiple of a complete rotation. As an example, a ship directly off of your port (left) bow is at course 250 mark 0. Directly behind you is 500 mark 0. Starboard (right) is at course 750 mark 0. Course 1000 is equivalent to 0 and is directly ahead.
