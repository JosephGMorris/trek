Operations Orientation 03

Transporters

When a station, planet, or ship has had a detailed scan performed, it is registered with the transportation computers and you are able to select it as a transportation target, provided you are within range (10,000 km for most Federation starships, when full power is passed to the system).

The range of the transporters may be extended by having your engineer set a higher power level to their system.

You will have the option of transporting either Crew or Cargo--each uses a different transportation subsystem. If transporting cargo, you have the option of setting the target destination to any of your cargo bays. Crew may only be sent to the transporter room.

Once you have selected a target, engage the transporter by activating the buffer control stips (click near the top of the bars), and wait for the cycle to complete. If a problem is encountered during a transporter cycle, a notice will appear explaining the problem, and the transport will be aborted.

Transport beams can be disrupted by shields and other energy interference, so make sure your at least one of your ship's shields is down during a transport.
