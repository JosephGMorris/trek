Engineering Orientation 01

Overview

A ship's Chief Engineering officer is perhaps the most important member of the bridge crew, as all other functions depend on the engineer to keep the ship flying.

From the engineering screen, you are able to quickly scan the status of all of the ships systems to see whether they are active, online, or damaged.

From the power distribution screen, you can transmit and rebalance the ship's power output to different systems as needed for the effective operation of the ship.

A word of caution: The engineer is one of the few officers in a position to cause accidental damage to a ship's systems if they are are not careful in their duties. Although there are many safeties in place, the engineer has the authority to by-pass all of them.
