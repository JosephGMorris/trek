Science Orientation 01

Overview

As a ship of exploration, the science officer is crucial to the mission of any Federation starship.

Many different types of scanners are required to get an accurate sense of what is happening in deep space. The primary distinction is between long and short range scanners (those which use subspace or other means to gather information beyond the local range of light, and those that do not).

In addition to the standard instrumentation, operational scanners, such as high-resolution scanning equipment, and internal environmental scanners are also operated and supervised by the science officer.
